Laxminarayan Steel Industries, established in 2008, is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality stainless steel fasteners based in Wadhwan City, Surendranagar, Gujarat.

Raw Materials

PREMIUM STEEL
GRADES

We use only certified, premium-grade stainless steel to ensure maximum durability and performance across all applications.

204Cu
SS Grade 204Cu

A copper-bearing austenitic grade offering enhanced cold-forming and heading properties. The added copper improves ductility and machinability, making it ideal for precision fastener manufacturing with good corrosion resistance at a competitive cost.

304
SS Grade 304

The most widely used stainless steel grade, offering excellent corrosion resistance, weldability, and surface finish quality — suitable for food processing, pharmaceutical, marine, and heavy industrial applications.

316
SS Grade 316

The marine-grade alloy with added molybdenum for superior resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and chemical exposure — preferred for harsh chloride-rich and high-salinity environments.
